ENTERPRISE LEADERSHIP

SFx CRM Transformation — Cox Automotive

Cox Automotive operates some of the most recognized brands in the automotive industry — Autotrader, Kelley Blue Book, and Dealertrack. Unifying 12+ business units onto a single enterprise Salesforce platform was one of the most complex CRM transformations in the automotive technology sector.

As Senior Manager of Software Engineering and sole technical decision authority, I sponsored and delivered this six-year program end-to-end — from architecture design through full enterprise deployment and two years of enhancements.

I held full accountability for all technical and architectural decisions on this program. Key decisions made and signed off independently:
  • Authentication framework — Bridge authentication for GDPR/CCPA compliance across all 12 units
  • Integration architecture — Genesys-driven email-to-case routing
  • Data model standardization enterprise-wide
  • Referrals Package re-architecture enabling cross-team lead sharing
  • Copado deployment automation framework
  • 30+ Power BI dashboards for executive-level visibility

SPARK RECOGNITIONS

Building Enterprise-Grade Engineering at Scale

Over 11+ years at Cox Automotive, I have grown from Senior Salesforce Developer to Senior Manager of Software Engineering — building, scaling, and transforming one of the most complex enterprise technology portfolios in the automotive industry.

My leadership spans the full engineering lifecycle: standing up new teams from scratch, migrating legacy platforms with zero downtime, and driving digital transformation initiatives that directly impact thousands of users and millions in revenue. Notable highlights beyond SFx include:

Leading the FleetNet Migration — building a brand new team and consolidating two core Cox businesses onto a unified Salesforce platform, eliminating system redundancies and delivering significant operational cost savings.

Delivering the NADA Placemat mobile application, enabling real-time booth management, lead capture, and customer engagement tracking for 100+ booths at major industry events — improving event ROI by 60%.

Pioneering Agentforce AI at Cox Automotive by leading a hackathon team that designed autonomous conversational AI agents for the service team, reducing workload by 40% while preserving seamless human escalation for complex cases.

Architecting 20+ SAP-to-Salesforce integrations, an Oracle CPQ implementation that cut quote creation time by 70%, MuleSoft-driven real-time data synchronisation, and a complete Salesforce re-implementation — all supporting 6,000+ users across the enterprise.

Across every initiative, the constant has been the same: build the right team, make the right architectural decisions, and deliver technology that creates lasting business value.
