Developing People

Great teams are not hired — they are built. Over ten years leading globally distributed engineering teams, I have developed 170+ technology professionals through a simple but powerful philosophy: hire for aptitude, coach for everything else.

I deliberately recruit smart minds from adjacent technologies, invest in platform-specific training, and create real opportunities for underrepresented professionals to lead high-stakes work. The results speak for themselves.

My Coaching Philosophy

Technical and logical aptitude matters far more than platform familiarity. When I identify someone with strong foundational thinking, I take a chance on them regardless of their current stack — then coach them deliberately toward the right direction. This approach extends especially to underrepresented professionals, particularly women who possess exceptional capability but lack the exposure or confidence to lead.

What This Looks Like in Practice

AI Readiness

As AI reshapes how engineering teams operate, I led by example — personally investing in AI research, getting certified in agentic AI capabilities, and then systematically coaching my entire team through the same journey. The result: one of the first fully AI-ready engineering units at Cox Automotive.
