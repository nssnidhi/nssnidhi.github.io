Authority Magazine — C-Suite Perspectives on AI: Where to Use AI and Where to Rely Only on Humans December 2025 | Authority Magazine Editorial Staff

In this C-Suite Perspectives feature, I share my leadership philosophy on one of the most critical decisions facing technology executives today: where AI creates real value and where human judgment must remain non-negotiable. Drawing on 15+ years leading enterprise AI transformation, I outline five core principles—from responsible innovation and data security to designing seamless human-AI handoffs and supporting teams through change.

A central theme is that AI is not a competitor to human capability but a companion that amplifies it when designed with intention and empathy. “Human judgment remains essential wherever trust, empathy, and creativity are required — such as strategic decision-making, complex customer interactions, and mentoring. These are not tasks to automate; they define what it means to be human.”
